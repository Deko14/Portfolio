ABOUT

Real name Ivan Kostadinov, born in Skopje-Macedonia known for his stage name THE SMOKE HOUSE, he started producing beats and making music at an early age in the year of 2010. As the times flew by, 8 years later Ivan realised he was more than just a producer and his inner artist in himself woke up to dominate the Macedonian music stage with his unique bumpy hiphop/trap sound. He also makes music in English, which also proves that his music and talent can reach a global potential. THE SMOKE HOUSE and his team are working on many new projects like singles, mixtapes, albums, music videos and more. He is stll an unsigned artist and open for business at anytime. Make sure to check his music on all streaming platforms.
